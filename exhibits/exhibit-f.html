INVESTIGATION REPORT

EXHIBIT F — HSBC IDAM Training: Rapid Fix Under Security Deadline

Corroborated · Security Compliance · August 2022
Section 1

Background

HSBC was GP Strategies' #1 client by revenue. In August 2022, HSBC's security team identified issues with exposed credentials in their IDAM (Identity and Access Management) training completion reporting. This required rapid courseware fixes under strict compliance deadlines.

HSBC operated under strict security compliance requirements. The exposed credentials issue required immediate remediation. The team developed, tested, and delivered fixes that were approved by HSBC's LMS team. Files were Simple Framework format, archived to DevOps rather than LCH per established protocol.
